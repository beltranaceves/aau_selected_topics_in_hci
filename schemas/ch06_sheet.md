# Case Studies 

    AMAZON DASH BUTTON
      - Simple IoT; internal components: MCU, WiFi, mic, battery.
      - WiFi setup: hotspot (Android), ultrasound (iOS).

    ANDROID TWEAKS WITHOUT ROOT
      - Netguard via VPN API; PixOff for AMOLED; kiosk reuse; automation risks.

    COVID CONTACT TRACING
      - BLE proximity IDs, decentralized privacy model.

    AIRTAGS & FIND MY
      - BLE for encrypted location relay, NFC identification, UWB precision.
      - Issue: stalking; protections vary by platform.

    MOBILE CARD EMULATION
      - HCE (software, less secure), SEM (hardware, secure).
      - Applications: payments, ID cards.

    COMPUTATIONAL PHOTOGRAPHY
      - Artificial bokeh, Night Sight.
      - Superresolution from multi-frame bursts.
      - Risks of AI hallucination.
